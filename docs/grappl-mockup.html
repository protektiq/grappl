Capabilities

Built on a
serious stack

01
Vision Inference

A custom-trained Roboflow model detects positions, submission attempts, and transitions frame by frame — calibrated for the chaos of live grappling footage.

Roboflow
02
Coaching Narrative

LangChain transforms raw detection events into structured, coaching-grade analysis — not just timestamps, but context, pattern recognition, and specific corrective feedback.

LangChain + Claude
03
Auto Clipping

Every detected event triggers an automatic FFmpeg clip, trimmed around the event window and stored in your personal film library. No manual scrubbing.

FFmpeg Pipeline
04
Schema Evolution

As your detection vocabulary grows — new positions, leg locks, wrestling sequences — OpenRewrite migrates your entire history without breaking a single record.

OpenRewrite
05
Artifact Signing

Every processed clip and inference result is signed with Sigstore, giving you a verified chain of custody on your film library from day one.

Sigstore
06
Container Orchestration

The entire pipeline runs as isolated Kubernetes services on Minikube — each stage independently scalable, observable, and ready to graduate to cloud infrastructure.

Kubernetes + Minikube
